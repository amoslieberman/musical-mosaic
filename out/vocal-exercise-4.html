Vocal Exercise 4

Chromatic interval training: ascending and descending with expanding range from tonic

Exercise Overview

Pattern

Chromatic interval training through all semitones:

Ascending: Play root, go up chromatically to max, jump back to root. Increase max by 1 semitone. Repeat until octave.
Descending: Same pattern from octave, decreasing max by 1 semitone until reaching ♭2
